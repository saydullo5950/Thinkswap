Academic integrity

Students are required to always deal ethically with work on Thinkswap.

Using prior examples of student work is an amazing way to study and improve, however all students using Thinkswap are required to act with academic integrity.

Student education programmes, collaboration with university academics, watermarking, and submission of all documents to plagiarism detection tools are amongst the many ways we protect student work on Thinkswap.
